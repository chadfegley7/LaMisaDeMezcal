Cocktails de Mezcal

Mezcal Madness

Ingredients
・1 oz. Mezcal
・3/4 oz. Aperol
・1/2 oz. Fresh Lemon Juice
・2-3 dashes Angostura Bitters
・3 oz. Aperol
・Lemon Peel for Garnish

Directions
Fill cocktail shaker with ice. Add mezcal, aperol, lemon juice, and bitters. Shake until well chilled, about 15 seconds. Fill rocks glass with fresh ice. Add club soda to glass and strain cocktail on top. Garnish with lemon peel.
